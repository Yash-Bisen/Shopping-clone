Biography of Mahendra Singh Dhoni:-Mahendra Singh Dhoni is still the most loved cricketer of all times in India; he is a celebrity, only behind Sachin Tendulkar in popularity.

He has also received the Man of the Series and the Man of the Match Awards on several occasions. It was Sri Lanka in India ODI Series in 2005-06 where he received his first Man of the Series Award.

Dhoni’s latest acquisition which cost him 29 lakhs is the street-legal version of the track-only H2R; the H2 has a supercharged 998cc motor that produces a mammoth 197bhp with 133Nm of torque.
